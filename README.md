# ⚖️ THEMIS – AI-Powered Credit Risk Intelligence Platform

## Overview

THEMIS is an AI-driven Credit Risk Intelligence Platform designed to assist financial institutions, lending organizations, risk analysts, and credit officers in evaluating borrower creditworthiness through machine learning and explainable AI.

The platform analyzes demographic, financial, employment, and credit behavior characteristics to generate intelligent lending decisions, risk classifications, explainability insights, and portfolio-level analytics.

---

## Key Features

### Credit Risk Assessment

* Predicts borrower creditworthiness using Machine Learning.
* Generates approval probability scores.
* Produces intelligent credit decisions.
* Calculates credit risk scores.

### Explainable AI

* Identifies key risk drivers influencing model decisions.
* Provides transparent decision explanations.
* Supports model interpretability and governance.

### Portfolio Analytics

* Loan portfolio distribution analysis.
* Risk segmentation and concentration analysis.
* Demographic and customer trend analysis.
* Lending exposure visualization.

### Model Performance Monitoring

* Accuracy tracking.
* Precision, Recall, and F1 Score evaluation.
* Confusion Matrix visualization.
* Model validation insights.

### Automated PDF Reporting

* Professional Greek-inspired risk assessment reports.
* Applicant profile summaries.
* Risk classification.
* AI-generated interpretation.
* Executive decision support documentation.
